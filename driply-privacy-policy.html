Driply

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Driply ("the app," "we," "us") is an independent app. This page explains how the app handles information, in plain terms.

The short version

Driply does not collect, transmit, or store any of your information on our servers — or anyone else's. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Information we collect

Driply does not collect any personal information. Everything you enter into the app — subscription names, amounts, billing cycles, renewal dates, categories, and "last used" dates — is stored only in your device's local storage. We never see it, receive it, or have any way to access it.

Notifications

If you allow it, Driply schedules local reminders on your device for upcoming renewals. These reminders are created and stored entirely on your phone — no notification content, schedule, or subscription data is sent to us or to Apple's push servers.

Third-party services

Driply does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services of any kind. The app works fully offline. The CSV export feature uses your device's built-in share sheet to let you save or send a file — this happens entirely on your device and does not pass through our servers.

Data sharing

Because we don't collect any data, there is nothing for us to share, sell, or disclose to third parties.

Your control over your data

You can edit or delete any entry within the app at any time. Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all data stored by Driply, since nothing exists outside of it.

Children's privacy

Driply is not directed at children under 13. Since the app does not collect information from any user, it does not knowingly collect information from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to stopthedrip.app@gmail.com.
